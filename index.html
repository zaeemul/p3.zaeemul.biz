So what's this all about?

Tweakker revolutionizes the writing and updating of your social media sites. Now everyone has something smart and funny to say! Tweakker is a tool that breaks and numbers a block of text for you. Your Tweakker can be tweet- or any other length that you choose to meet your social media site’s needs.
Tweakker allows everyone to be a great tweet and Facebook status writer. Tweakker frees up information from across the web. Quote from your favorite author’s book, live tweet from Wikipedia entries, organize or automate your posts and updates.
